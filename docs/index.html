⛽ Weekly Fuel Prices & Changes

📌 Fuel prices are set weekly by the Malaysian government (Automatic Pricing Mechanism). RON95 is a market-priced fuel. BUDI RON95 is the subsidised price (RM 1.88 off) for eligible Malaysians — you need to register your IC/MyKad with Setel or at a petrol station to get this rate.
